How to Purchase Your Vehicle

Necessary Details and Documents

Before you start, ensure you have the following ready:

  • National ID / Company Registration
  • KRA PIN Certificate
  • Contact Number
  • Email ID
  • Active NTSA TIMS Account

Purchase Process

Select Your Vehicle

Browse our selection and connect with a sales representative via our website, WhatsApp, hotline, or visit us in person.

Book an Inspection

Schedule an in-yard inspection to ensure the vehicle meets your standards and requirements.

Verification

  • Check the vehicle's mileage
  • Verify logbook details
  • Confirm the seller's bank details

Quotation

Request a proforma invoice from our sales team.

Confirmation

Finalize the payment transfer and share the transfer confirmation with us.

Documentation

  • Finalize sales agreements
  • Complete the ownership transfer through your active NTSA TIMS account
  • Receive the original logbook from the seller

Release

  • Prepare the vehicle, ensuring all necessary accessories and tools are in order
  • Complete a car wash and detailing
  • Hand over the vehicle to you, the proud new owner

Asset Finance

Necessary Details and Documents

To apply for asset finance, have these documents ready:

  • Bank Statements (Last 6 months)
  • Mpesa Statements (Last 6 months)
  • Employment / Business Confirmation Documents
  • Salary Slips (Last 6 months)
  • National ID / Company Registration
  • KRA PIN Certificate
  • Contact Number
  • Email ID
  • Active NTSA TIMS Account

Eligibility and Approval Process

Check Eligibility

Confirm eligibility with your preferred financiers, whether it's a bank, SACCO, or microfinance institution. Our sales agents are available to assist.

Submit Documents

Provide all necessary documents to your chosen financier and obtain confirmation of your finance eligibility.

Vehicle Reservation

Reserve your vehicle with a down payment.

Finance Approval

Work with your financier to get approval for your loan.

Undertaking / Release Letter

  • Complete your portion of the payment
  • The seller will finalize the ownership transfer and submit any remaining documents
